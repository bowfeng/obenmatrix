# Research

Conduct thorough research on topics, review literature, and synthesize knowledge.

## When to Use

- User asks for literature reviews or background research
- Need to summarize academic papers or technical documents
- Synthesizing information from multiple sources
- Preparing research reports

## Capabilities

- Search and analyze academic papers (arXiv, PubMed, etc.)
- Conduct systematic literature reviews
- Summarize technical documents
- Extract key findings and methodologies
- Compare different approaches and perspectives
- Create research reports and annotations
- Manage citation databases
